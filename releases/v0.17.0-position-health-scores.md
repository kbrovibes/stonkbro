# v0.17.0 — Position Health Scores

**Date:** 2026-04-26

## What changed

Added a per-position health scoring system that evaluates each position across three weighted dimensions.

### New: `src/lib/options/health-score.ts`

**Three dimensions:**

| Dimension | Weight | What it measures |
|-----------|--------|------------------|
| DTE Urgency | 40% | Time decay pressure on short legs |
| Strike Risk | 35% | Distance from stock price to short strike |
| Profit Progress | 25% | Premium captured vs entry price |

**Status levels:**

| Score | Status | Color |
|-------|--------|-------|
| 70-100 | Healthy | Emerald |
| 45-69 | Watch | Amber |
| 25-44 | Danger | Orange |
| 0-24 | Critical | Red |

### Updated: `/api/signals`

Response now includes `healthScores` array with a `PositionHealth` object per active position.
